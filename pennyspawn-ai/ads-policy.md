# Advertising policy

- Ads run only on a commercial deployment, never on GitHub Pages preview mode.
- The site owner must obtain approval from the ad provider.
- Visitors may refuse advertising cookies and continue using the local app.
- Never click your own ads or ask other people to click them.
- Never use bots, auto-refresh, paid-to-click, traffic exchanges, or fabricated impressions.
- Never reward ad views with cash, cryptocurrency, gift cards, or other transferable value.
- Revenue figures are not shown unless verified by the ad provider.
